Medea Kakachia, a native of Zugdidi, Georgia, became a Colorado resident in 2024. Since then, she has been transitioning into the accounting profession and is currently pursuing her path toward becoming a Certified Public Accountant (CPA). She works as an accountant in Denver and brings strong expertise in financial management and accountability.
Medea’s multicultural journey spans Georgia, Ukraine, and the United States. She studied for her Medical Doctor degree in Vinnytsia, Ukraine, and Tbilisi Georgia, and later received a Master of Public Administration degree in Denver, Colorado. Throughout her career, she has worked with U.S., European, and United Nations organizations. Most notably, she served with the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) in Tbilisi, Georgia, where she managed large-scale programs in education, workforce development, and social protection.
Through the Colorado Georgian Community Association, Medea is committed to supporting and strengthening the Georgian community by contributing her experience in project management and nonprofit development.
